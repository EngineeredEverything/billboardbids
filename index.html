Frequently Asked Questions

What is BillboardBids?

BillboardBids is the leading online marketplace for billboard advertising. We connect advertisers directly with billboard owners, eliminating middlemen and reducing costs by up to 89%. Search over 12,000 billboards across 500+ cities and place competitive bids on premium outdoor advertising locations.

How do billboard auctions work?

Our auction system is simple: 1) Search for billboards by location, size, or type. 2) Place your bid with your desired price and campaign dates. 3) Compete fairly with other advertisers. 4) Win the auction and launch your campaign. Bids are secured with payment authorization, ensuring serious advertisers only.

How much does billboard advertising cost?

Billboard costs vary by location, size, and demand. Digital billboards in prime locations like Times Square can cost $15,000+/month, while highway billboards average $4,000-8,000/month. Local billboards can start as low as $750/month. BillboardBids users save an average of 89% compared to traditional agency pricing.

Who can use BillboardBids?

BillboardBids serves two audiences: Advertisers (businesses, agencies, brands) who want to purchase billboard space, and Billboard Owners who want to monetize their outdoor advertising inventory. Signing up is free for both.
